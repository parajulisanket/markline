Part-time job types commonly available to students.

Students in the UK can find a variety of part-time job opportunities that fit around their study schedules. Common roles include retail assistants, baristas, waitstaff, library helpers, delivery riders, administrative assistants, and on-campus jobs like student ambassadors or research assistants.
